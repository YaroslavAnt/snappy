Frequently Asked Questions

iCloud is not working / Takes very long to sync (iOS/OSX)

Syncing with iCloud can take a long time, from a few minutes to hours. The 2 main factors that influence this are the numbers of snaps and the internet connection.

To exclude the obvious, please make sure to check if iCloud is enabled on all devices.

Solution: TBA. Unfortunately this process is out of our hands and is completely handled by the Apple servers.

The top menu for Snappy is completely inactive and I can't create new snaps

This can occasionally happen if you use multiple monitors. We are still investigating the cause and will provide a permanent fix in one of the updates.

Solution: Close Snappy from the Activity Monitor and open it again

With iCloud enabled, Snappy crashes a few seconds after it was opened

This happens because of an inconsistency between your local snap database version and the iCloud version.

Temporary solution: Before the app crashes, open the Preferences panel, uncheck the "Use iCloud storage" checkbox and exit the App via "Quit Snappy".
