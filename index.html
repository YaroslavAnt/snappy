We call them snaps for short. A snap is always-on-top and allows you to annotate, resize, change opacity and more.

Collect and Organize

One of Snappy's key aspects is centered around collecting pieces of content (snaps), which are securely stored on your computer and neatly organized.

Share and Collaborate

Self Destruct / Password Protect / Encrypted

Sharing a snap is simple, secure and fast. With SnappyLink you get a unique URL to send around, visible on all devices.

Aditionally, you can set a password for your shared snap and/or set a self-destruct timer on it.
